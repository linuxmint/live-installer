A great community

  • The Linux Mint community is one of the best on the Internet. Our users not only provide most of our funding, but also ideas, artwork, and sometimes even source code.
  • Linux Mint users are happy to share their passion, enthusiasm, and eagerness to help. Don't hesitate to ask questions in the forums or get involved with the community.
  • Send us your feedback and tell us about your experience. We'll listen to your ideas and use them to further improve Linux Mint.
