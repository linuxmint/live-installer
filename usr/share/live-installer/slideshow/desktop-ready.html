Out of the box

  • Firefox, Thunderbird, LibreOffice and Quassel come pre-installed in Linux Mint, so you can get started immediately with the desktop and the Internet.
  • Unlike other Linux distributions Linux Mint is also ready to play mp3s, DVDs and Flash video as soon as you run it.
  • Other popular applications such as Skype, Picasa, Google Earth, Google Chrome and Opera can be installed with a click of the mouse.
  • Linux Mint is compatible with most popular file formats: zip, doc, xls, pdf, rar, mp3, wmv, mpg, mp4, mov ...etc
