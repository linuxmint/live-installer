Safe updates

  • Some operating systems make updating your system harder than it really needs to be. Linux Mint thinks upgrades should be quick and easy, and then get out of the way.
  • The Linux Mint Update Manager sits discreetly in your system tray and lets you know when updates are available. You don't have to apply updates when you don't want to. The Update Manager won't pop up or annoy you in any way.
  • All updated files are maintained and closely checked by trusted developers and maintainers.
  • All files are stored in special secure repositories for your use.
