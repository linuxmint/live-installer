Out of the box

  • Firefox, Thunderbird, LibreOffice and Quassel come pre-installed in Linux Mint, so you can get started immediately with your day to day routine on your desktop and the Internet.
  • Other popular applications such as Skype, Picasa, Google Earth and Opera can be installed with a click of the mouse.
  • Linux Mint is compatible with most popular file formats, such as: zip, doc, xls, pdf, rar, mp3, wmv, mpg, mp4, mov - no need download any extra software .
